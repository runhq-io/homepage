# How to Deploy

## Staging

Push to `master`. Digital Ocean auto-deploys to staging.

- **URL:** https://console-staging.runhq.io
- **Deploy activity:** https://cloud.digitalocean.com/apps/a6da8e9b-acfe-4908-9179-96f8ac432e0e/deployments?i=75ce49
- **DO app name:** be-runhq-staging

## Production

Push to `master`, then manually trigger "Deploy" from Digital Ocean.

- **URL:** https://console.runhq.io
- **DO app (runhq-be):** https://cloud.digitalocean.com/apps/2c67af4d-ebf8-4c60-b21c-b6c42831df11?i=75ce49
